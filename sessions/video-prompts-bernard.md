## MOTION TOKEN — [MOTION]

**[MOTION]** = Limited cut-out / paper-puppet animation, NOT fluid full
animation. Hold the flat 2D hand-drawn painterly look exactly — no realism
drift, no style change, no morphing. Keep characters mostly static "puppets"
with only ONE or TWO isolated moving parts. Allowed motion only: slow camera
push-in / pull-out / gentle pan or parallax across the painted background;
subtle character micro-motion (small head turn/tilt, a swinging stick-limb,
shoulders rising/falling with breath, small mouth/face movement, a slow blink);
the stylized BLUE tear-streaks slowly welling or sliding down; environmental
ambience (fire flicker + ember drift, lamp/lantern glow pulsing, wind moving
grass/dust/hair/cloth, falling snow/rain, drifting smoke, distant haze).
EXPLICITLY AVOID fluid lifelike body animation, walk cycles, running, limb
morphing, and 3D camera fly-throughs. Duration ~3–5 s per clip.

Each prompt below = **[MOTION]** + beat-specific movement (camera move +
character micro-action + ambient motion + duration).
